01 · About

About me

I'm a data analyst with an MBA in Marketing & Business Analytics, currently completing a Master of Data Science at Swinburne University in Melbourne. My background sits at the intersection of business strategy and data — I spent close to three years as a Customer Insights Analyst working on segmentation for an apparel brand, and I'm now sharpening the technical side of the craft.

I work primarily in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau, with experience in Google Analytics and Excel for marketing-side analysis. I care about clean assumptions, reproducible work, and writing the README.

My journey into data

My route into data analytics ran through marketing. During my MBA at Bangalore University I specialised in Marketing & Business Analytics, then joined Keygap Software Solutions in Bangalore as a Customer Insights Analyst. For two and a half years I worked on customer segmentation for an apparel brand client — figuring out who the customers were, what they bought, and how the marketing team should talk to them.

That work made one thing obvious: I enjoyed the data side more than the campaign side. The questions that mattered — which segment is actually growing? which signup cohort retains? what does this dashboard not tell us? — were data questions. So I moved to Melbourne and started a Master of Data Science at Swinburne to build the technical foundation I'd been working around. The career change isn't really a pivot — it's the part of my last job finally getting its own title.

"I don't want to just produce reports. I want the report to change what the team does on Monday."
